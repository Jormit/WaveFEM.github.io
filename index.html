Applications

WaveFEM is designed to handle a wide range of electromagnetic simulation challenges across multiple industries and research domains.

High-Speed PCB Design

Analyze signal integrity, crosstalk, and transmission lines in multi-layer PCBs for high-speed digital applications.

Semiconductor Design

Simulate MMICs, RF chips, and packaging interconnects with accurate parasitic modeling and high-frequency performance analysis.

Antenna Design

Design patch antennas and antenna arrays with radiation pattern prediction and array analysis.

Waveguide Components

Simulate bandpass filters and multiplexers with precise S-parameter calculations for satellite and radar systems.
