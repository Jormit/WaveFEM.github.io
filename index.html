State-of-the-Art Accuracy

WaveFEM delivers exceptional accuracy across a wide range of electromagnetic simulation scenarios. Our advanced finite element method implementation consistently produces results that match or exceed those from industry-standard commercial software packages. The validation examples below demonstrate our solver's precision in handling complex geometries, material properties, and frequency-dependent behaviors.

Our simulation engine incorporates sophisticated numerical techniques including higher-order basis functions, adaptive mesh refinement, and robust error estimation algorithms to ensure reliable convergence and accurate field solutions. These capabilities enable engineers to trust WaveFEM for critical design decisions in demanding applications.

Meandering Microstrip Line on PCB

This validation demonstrates WaveFEM's capability in handling planar transmission line structures on printed circuit boards. The meandering microstrip presents challenges including substrate effects, conductor losses, and complex current distributions around bends and discontinuities.

S-Parameters from 3 - 5 GHz

The frequency response comparison shows WaveFEM accurately captures the transmission characteristics, including insertion loss, return loss, and phase response of the microstrip structure.

E field magnitude at 5GHz (WaveFEM)

The field visualization reveals proper electromagnetic field confinement around the microstrip conductor and realistic field enhancement at discontinuities and corners.
